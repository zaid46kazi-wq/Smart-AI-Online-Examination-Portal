# Student Guide: One-Time Exam Attempts

## ⚠️ Important: One Attempt Per Exam

Each student can attempt **every exam only once**. Choose when to take the exam wisely!

---

## Before You Start an Exam

### ✅ Check These Things:

1. **Internet Connection** - Strong and stable
2. **Device Camera** - Webcam is working and unblocked
3. **Microphone** - Mic is working (if required)
4. **Environment** - Quiet place where you won't be interrupted
5. **Time** - Have plenty of time available (don't rush!)
6. **Device Battery** - Fully charged or plugged in

---

## How the System Works

### Available Exams vs Attempted Exams

#### 🔵 Blue "Start Now" Button
- Exam is **available for attempt**
- Click to begin
- You have **one attempt**

#### 🟠 Grey "Already Attempted" Button
- You have already attempted this exam  
- **Cannot attempt again**
- Your score is saved and visible in your results

---

## What Happens During the Exam

### ✅ Allowed Actions:
- Read questions carefully
- Select your answers
- Review your answers (if time permits)
- Request help from invigilator (if available)
- Take notes on paper (if provided)

### ❌ NOT Allowed Actions:
- Switch to another tab/window
- Exit fullscreen (or lose focus)
- Copy/paste text
- Take screenshots
- Use right-click menu
- Use developer tools (F12)
- Use keyboard printing screen key
- Access outside help or materials (unless exam allows)

### ⚠️ Proctoring Warnings

You'll get **3 warnings max** for:
- Looking away from screen
- Multiple faces detected in cam  
- Absence from frame
- Switching tabs/windows
- Exiting fullscreen

**After 3 warnings → Auto-submit** (with whatever answers you have)

---

## Questions During Exam

### Navigation:
- Use **Question Navigator** on left side
- Quick navigation between questions
- Visited questions change color

### Question Types:
- Multiple choice (select ONE option)
- Each question has marks
- Partial marking may apply

### Marking:
- Green ✅ = Attempted
- Orange ⏱️ = Not answered yet
- Red 🔴 = Reviewed/flagged

---

## Submitting Your Exam

### Manual Submit:
1. Answer all questions (or skip if allowed)
2. Click **"Submit Exam"** button (top right)
3. Confirm submission
4. Exam locks immediately

### Auto-Submit:
- **Time expires** → Auto-submitted
- **3 proctoring violations** → Auto-submitted  
- **Unreliable connection** → May auto-submit

### After Submit:
- Cannot change answers
- Cannot retake exam
- Results show immediately OR within 24 hours

---

## After Exam

### View Results:

1. Go to **Student Dashboard**
2. Scroll to **"Your Results"** section
3. Find your exam in the list
4. See: Score, Marks, Percentage, Pass/Fail

### Download Certificate:
- Click **"PDF"** button next to result
- Save as certificate/proof
- Share with others if needed

## What if the System Detects Cheating?

### Proctoring Violations:
- **1st violation**: Warning ⚠️
- **2nd violation**: Warning + message ⚠️
- **3rd violation**: Exam auto-submitted 🛑

### Serious Violations:
- Detected by AI monitoring
- Logged and reported to admin
- May result in exam cancellation
- May affect your academic record

### Appeal Process:
If you believe you got violation unfairly:
1. Contact your instructor immediately
2. Explain the situation
3. Provide context/evidence if possible
4. Admin will review and decide

## Troubleshooting

### Cannot Start Exam?
```
❌ "Exam Already Attempted"
→ You already completed this exam
→ Cannot retake

❌ "Unauthorized"  
→ You don't have permission to take this exam
→ Contact your instructor

❌ Connection Error
→ Check your internet
→ Refresh and try again
```

### Lost Connection During Exam?
- **Auto-save**: Answers saved every 30 seconds
- **Reconnect**: Refresh page to resume
- **Backup**: Last saved answers recover
- **Auto-submit**: After 5 min disconnection

### Proctoring Keeps Warning Me?
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Keep camera at eye level
- Don't eat/drink during exam
- Don't wear sunglasses
- Position yourself in frame properly
- Use stable background (no moving objects)

## Best Practices

### ✅ DO:
- Take practice tests first
- Plan your exam time
- Start with easy questions
- Review difficult questions last
- Make sure camera/mic working
- Close unnecessary applications
- Have water nearby
- Relax and breathe

### ❌ DON'T:
- Rush through questions
- Leave questions unanswered (if not required)
- Try to fix answers last second
- Don't panic if you don't know answer
- Don't look away frequently  
- Don't have multiple windows open
- Don't eat/drink while exam running
- Don't use unauthorized materials
